About Us

ISABE promotes global knowledge exchange on airbreathing propulsion for flight vehicles. Its biennial conferences, featuring technical sessions, forums, social gatherings, and invited lectures, foster informal discussions, networking, and relaxation. Visit ISABE's Conferences & Events page for updates and contact your national representative with any questions as a member.



ISABE Objectives

  • To encourage research and participation in the field of airbreathing propulsion.
  • To participate in international projects of development in the field of airbreathing engines.
  • To organize an International Conference on Airbreathing Engines every two years.

Membership

Membership Process
To join ISABE, prospective members must submit an application sponsored by their national Primary Delegate and approved by the Board of Directors. International organizations require joint applications from all sponsoring nations.

Eligibility
Individuals worldwide with an active interest in airbreathing propulsion for flight vehicles can become individual members. Universities, colleges, research labs, and airbreathing engine manufacturers and users are eligible for organizational membership.
