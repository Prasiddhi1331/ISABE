Invitation

Welcome to the 26th International Society for Air Breathing Engines (ISABE) Conference being held in the vibrant city of Toulouse, which stands as France's capital for aerospace technology and innovation. Toulouse's strategic location has made it a major hub for the aerospace industry, and we are thrilled to gather here with experts and enthusiasts from around the world to exchange knowledge and insights in this exciting field.
